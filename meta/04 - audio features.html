AudioSet: simultaneous labels¶

Meta-analysis of models that include all AudioSet labels (i.e., in the same model). This is out of date (no Budapest dataset) and not terribly interesting—the main takeaway is that the weird “whistling” and “wood” results observed above persist (presumably due to unaccounted-for visual or other confounding).

ids = ["9EdG8", "MGvN3", "AqQnO", "Ma41J", "wjNQX", "97pkB", "wkV43", "M4j8z", "A25gR"]

effects = sorted(['as-Animal', 'as-Noise', 'as-Silence', 'as-Hands', 'as-Vehicle',
                  'as-Whistling','as-Water', 'as-Wood', 'as-Music', 'as-Tools', 'as-Wind'])

effects = {e: ids for e in effects}

process_set('audioset-all', effects, download=True, exclude=['sherlockPart1'])
